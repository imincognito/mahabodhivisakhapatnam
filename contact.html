Planning Your Visit

First-time Visitors

New to our center? Come 15 minutes early for orientation. No special clothing required - wear comfortable attire.

Parking Available

Free parking available on-site. Additional street parking nearby for special events and weekend gatherings.

Accessibility

Our center is wheelchair accessible with ramp access and accessible restroom facilities available.

What to Bring

Meditation cushions provided. You may bring your own cushion or mat. Water bottles and light snacks welcomed.
