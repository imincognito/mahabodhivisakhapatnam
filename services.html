Our Services

Comprehensive Spiritual Programs

Meditation Classes

Daily guided meditation sessions for beginners and advanced practitioners. Learn various techniques including mindfulness, loving-kindness, and concentration practices.

  • Morning meditation (6:00 AM - 7:00 AM)
  • Evening sessions (6:30 PM - 7:30 PM)
  • Weekend intensive workshops
  • Personal guidance available
Free for all

Dharma Teachings

Weekly dharma talks exploring Buddhist philosophy, ethics, and practical wisdom for navigating modern life challenges with Buddhist principles.

  • Sunday dharma talks (4:00 PM - 5:30 PM)
  • Study groups and discussions
  • Text-based learning sessions
  • Q&A with experienced teachers
Donation based

Spiritual Retreats

Immersive retreat experiences ranging from day-long to week-long programs. Deep dive into meditation practice and spiritual contemplation.

  • Monthly day retreats
  • Quarterly weekend retreats
  • Annual summer intensive
  • Silent meditation periods
₹500 - ₹5000

Healing Practices

Holistic healing approaches combining traditional Buddhist healing methods with modern wellness practices for mind, body, and spirit harmony.

  • Energy healing sessions
  • Stress reduction programs
  • Counseling and guidance
  • Group healing circles
₹200 - ₹1000

Youth Programs

Special programs designed for children and teenagers to introduce them to Buddhist values, meditation, and mindful living in an age-appropriate way.

  • Children's meditation (Ages 8-12)
  • Teen mindfulness groups (Ages 13-18)
  • Family meditation sessions
  • Creative workshops and activities
Free for families

Community Events

Regular community gatherings, festivals, and celebrations that bring together our sangha in the spirit of unity and shared spiritual growth.

  • Buddha Purnima celebrations
  • Monthly community dinners
  • Volunteer service projects
  • Cultural programs and talks
Community supported
